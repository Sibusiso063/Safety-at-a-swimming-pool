Guidelines

Introduction to your safety!

These are guidelines tips, things you should know and practice.

  • Respect lifeguard instruction and make sure that the life jackets ore flotation are available and in good condition.
  • Do not consume alcohol or any substances that could affect you while swimming.
  • Shower before entering the pool to help maintain clean water and reduce the spread of germs.
  • Wear sunscreen to protect you against sunburn.
  • Avoid holding your breath for extended periods or attempting stunts that could lead to accidents.
